# Legal Advice Boundary

This project is not legal advice.

Lex 4i does not:

- Provide legal conclusions
- Replace attorney judgment
- Predict case outcomes
- Confirm claim validity
- Create attorney-client relationships
- Access proprietary legal databases
- Decide whether a draft should be sent

The project demonstrates workflow infrastructure for organizing synthetic matter materials into structured, reviewable artifacts.

Any use with real matter materials would require appropriate confidentiality controls, jurisdiction-aware review, attorney supervision where required, and a clear human approval process.
