# Human Review Boundaries

Lex 4i is a matter organization harness. It does not replace human review.

This project is not legal advice.

Human/legal review is required for:

- Legal significance of facts
- Legal claim viability
- Legal strategy
- Statutes of limitation and deadlines
- Jurisdiction-specific rules
- Ethics obligations
- Privilege and confidentiality decisions
- Whether any draft should be sent
- Whether evidence is admissible, reliable, or complete
- Whether issue/risk categories are relevant to the matter

Generated artifacts should be treated as structured work product for inspection, not as final legal conclusions.
